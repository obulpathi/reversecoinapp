Terminology

With Reversecoin, you can create two different kinds of accounts: Standard Accounts and Reverse Accounts. Standard accounts behave very much like your Bitcoin accounts and allow you to send and receive money for daily purposes. Reverse accounts behave much like you bank savings account where you can deposit large amount of coins and keep them safe from hackers. Each vault account has a configurable timeout and is backed by two key pairs, one online and one offline. You only need online keypair to transfer coins from vault. When you transfer your coins using online keypair, your transactions get confirmed after they live for the timeout period in blockchain. If someone steals your online key pair and transfers coins, the transactions will have to wait in block chain for the timeout period. During which, you can use your offline key pair and reverse the transactions not initiated by you and restore your coins to other address. You can also use your offline key pair for immediate transfer of coins in your Reverse, instead of waiting for timeout period. In a sentence, Reverse account has the ease of use of hot wallets (online account) and security of cold wallets (offline accounts). We also provide a monitoring service, to send you real-time alerts, when coins are transferred from your addresses. Tutorial

Setup

Start your terminal
Start the reversecoin daemon using the command "reversecoind"
Start the reversecoinminer in another tab using the command "reversecoin-miner"

General commands

Display general information
$ rwallet info
Output of info

Display account information
$ rwallet account
Output of account

Display balance
$ rwallet balance
Output of balance

Display vault information
$ rwallet getvaults
Output of getvaults

Displays transactions received by an address
$ rwallet received
Output of received

Create a new address
$ rwallet newaddress
Output of newaddress

Send and receive coins using normal accounts

Send coins to an address
$ rwallet newaddress
Copy the address
$ rwallet send newaddress
Output of received
Wait for a minute or two for the confirmation
$ rwallet balance
Output of balance

Send and receive coins using vault accounts

First, deposit some amount into a vault
$ rwallet vault_send amount
Output of received
Check the balance of vault accounts
$ rwallet getvaults
Output of received

$ rwallet savings
Output of received

For normal withdraw using online keys only
$ rwallet vault_withdraw
Output of received

$ rwallet vault_pending.py Output of received

Get Account
Wait for pending transactions If you want the transactions to go through, just wait for the confirmation period Else,
For overriding the transactions from vault

$ rwallet vault_fast_override
